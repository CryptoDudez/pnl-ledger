Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

💚 We want you to be happy with P&L Ledger. If you're not satisfied, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all new subscriptions — no questions asked.

1. 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you subscribe to P&L Ledger Pro and are not satisfied for any reason, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial purchase. This applies to both monthly and annual plans.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund within the 7-day window, email us at stresstosuccses@gmail.com with:

  • Your registered email address
  • The date of purchase
  • A brief reason (optional but appreciated)

We will process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds are returned to the original payment method.

3. Renewal Charges

After the initial 7-day period, subscription charges are non-refundable. If you cancel your subscription, you will retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

4. Annual Subscriptions

Annual subscribers may request a full refund within 7 days of purchase. After 7 days, annual subscriptions are non-refundable but you will retain access for the full year.

5. Exceptions

Refunds may be granted outside the 7-day window at our sole discretion in exceptional circumstances such as:

  • Duplicate charges
  • Technical issues that prevented access to the service
  • Unauthorised charges

6. Cancellations

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings or by contacting us. Cancellation stops future charges but does not trigger an automatic refund for the current period.

7. Contact

For refund requests or billing questions: stresstosuccses@gmail.com

We typically respond within 24 hours on business days.
